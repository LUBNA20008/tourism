Popular destinations in Saudi Arabia

Home to the holy Kaaba and the Haram Mosque, Makkah is the spiritual heart of Islam and attracts millions of Muslims for Hajj and Umrah pilgrimages.

Known as the city of the Prophet Muhammad, Medina is home to the Prophet’s Mosque and holds immense religious significance.

Located in the southwest, Abha offers cool weather, beautiful landscapes, and the Asir National Park with its mountains and cultural heritage.
